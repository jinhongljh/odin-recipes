Chicken Stir Fry

Description

This chicken stir fry recipe is packed with veggies and a delicious sweet and savory soy, ginger, and garlic sauce that gets a kick from chili flakes. Serve with white or brown rice for a quick and easy weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 cups white rice
  • ⅔ cup low-sodium soy sauce, or to taste
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • ¼ teaspoon red chili flakes
  • 3 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil, divided
  • 1 head broccoli, broken into florets
  • 1 onion, cut into large chunks
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 (8 ounce) can sliced water chestnuts, drained
  • 1 green bell pepper, cut into matchsticks

Steps

  1. Stir the soy sauce, sugar, cornstarch, and spices together.
  2. Add the chicken and toss. Cover and marinate.
  3. Cook the vegetables and water chestnuts in sesame oil.
  4. Cook the marinated chicken, then stir in the veggies and reserved marinade.
  5. Boil until the chicken is fully cooked. Serve over rice.
